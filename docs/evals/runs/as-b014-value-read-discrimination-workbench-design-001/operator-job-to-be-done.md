# Operator Job To Be Done

## 30-second decision

When the operator opens the workbench, they should be able to decide within 30 seconds whether the current packet is ready for scoring, review, interpretation, design follow-up, or stop/defer.

## Questions the workbench must answer

1. What evaluation or comparison packet am I reviewing?
2. Do the required artifacts exist?
3. Are outputs captured for the relevant sides?
4. Is scoring blank, locked, interpreted, or blocked?
5. Are source identities hidden, unavailable, reviewed, or out of scope?
6. Does route/expert/SAFE-OUT/confidence context exist?
7. What claim boundary applies?
8. What is the next safe operator action?
9. What is explicitly not authorized?
10. Is the packet ready for scoring, review, interpretation, design, or stop?

## Operator outcome

The operator should leave the first screen with one safe next action and a clear understanding of what that action will produce and what it will not authorize.
