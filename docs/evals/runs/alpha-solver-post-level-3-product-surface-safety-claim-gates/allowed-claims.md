# Allowed Claims

Allowed claims are limited to narrow, non-promotional statements about this packet itself.

## Allowed packet-description claims

The following statements are allowed when kept materially equivalent and not expanded into readiness or quality claims:

- This docs-only packet defines safety and claim gates for future product-surface work.
- This packet lists blocked claims, allowed claims, evidence prerequisites, safety gates, non-promotion rules, UI copy limits, API response copy limits, and stop conditions.
- This packet selects `NO_FURTHER_PRODUCT_SURFACE_SAFETY_CLAIM_GATES_LANES_SELECTED`.
- This packet identifies `ALPHA-SOLVER-POST-LEVEL-3-PRODUCT-SURFACE-SAFETY-CLAIM-GATES-FIX-001` as the blocker fallback lane.
- Level 6 controls whether and how this packet is used.

## Required qualifiers

Every allowed claim must preserve these qualifiers:

- The packet is docs-only.
- The packet is non-promotional.
- The packet does not establish execution evidence.
- The packet does not authorize user-facing product-surface implementation.
- The packet does not authorize quality, benchmark, superiority, MVP, production, dashboard, `/v1/solve`, provider, billing, fallback, or readiness claims.

## Disallowed expansion

An allowed packet-description claim becomes disallowed if it implies that a product surface is approved, that evidence is promoted, that users may rely on output quality, or that any implementation work has been completed.
