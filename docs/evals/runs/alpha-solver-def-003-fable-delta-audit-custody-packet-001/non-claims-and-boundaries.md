# DEF-003 non-claims

This custody packet prohibits the following claims:

- Do not claim the original Fable audit exists in repo evidence unless repo inspection found a committed path and the operator later verifies provenance.
- Do not claim the audit is complete.
- Do not state or imply what the missing audit found.
- Do not infer model, provider, version, date, or tooling.
- Do not claim a replacement audit reconstructs, validates, confirms, recovers, or stands in for the missing original.
- Do not mix prompt-contract manual simulation evidence with product/runtime audit evidence.
- Do not claim this packet verifies anything beyond custody state.
- Do not claim DEF-003 is closed.
- Do not claim an extract is complete.
- Do not claim provider validation, benchmark validation, production readiness, runtime readiness, security/privacy completion, Alpha Solver superiority, or broad-user readiness.
